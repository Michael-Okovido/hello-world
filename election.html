On the 8th of October 2021, the electoral committee of First Oxford College brought out some nominees from SS3 class for various prefectship position like the head boy position, head girl position, sanitary position etc. This position wasnâ€™t awarded due to academic excellence instead was given due to individual capabilities.

The nominees were given a period from Monday to Wednesday to carry out their campaigns. The electoral bodies also assisted the nominees for their campaigns by printing flyers.

The election commenced on the 14th of October during the morning assembly at 7:45am. The nominees for the head boy and head girl position were called out to give their manifestos, general election was conducted for all prefectship position. Students were given ballot paper to cast their votes for their choice of leaders. Ballot paper from all students were placed in the ballot boxes and given to the electoral bodies to accumulate the results.

While the electoral bodies were accumulating the results suspense filled the entire environment. Later in the day all students gathered to hear the results from their votes. Assisting the electoral bodies in saying the results, the D.O.S of First Oxford College read out the results to all members of the school. Immediately the results were read the students were blissful because their choices of leaders won the election. The result states:
Head boy= Agbedia Oghenekevwe
Head girl= Oke Nice
Punctuality prefect= Okpowodu Rukevwe
Sanitary prefect= Ohwevwo Rita
Library prefect= Ajiri Anabelle
Laboratory prefect= Onoghofor Jennifer

The inauguration of the various position was announced by the D.O.S to be conducted on the 21st of October 2021.
