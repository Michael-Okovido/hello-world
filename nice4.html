THE GREAT TRANSFORMER

I heard rattling of drums from afar
Wonder welled up within me
Then like a cheetah, I dashed to the spot
A reveling crowd I saw
I glared and stared in amusement
Strand in white and maroon apparel
“What’s happening?! What’s happening?!”
I inquired from one amongst the crowd
“A Heroine! Heroine she’s being praised”
He replied
“Who is she? Where is she?” I asked further
“Here she comes, LOOK OVER THERE!!”
Someone shouted
Looking there,
A great goddess I saw
A true image of beauty
Ever willing camel, straight-laced
A statue of knowledge
A great statue filled with knowledge, vitality, wisdom and tranquility
Painted on her face was a passionate smile,
Love and care.
As brave as a lion
As kind as a mother
Words too scarce to describe her person
But here she comes, MRS. OKOLIN
The great transformer.
